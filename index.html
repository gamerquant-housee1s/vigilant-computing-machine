FAQ

Get answers to common questions about Windows 11 AI features.

Is Copilot free?

Yes, Microsoft Copilot is included with your Windows 11 update at no additional cost. Simply update to the latest version to access AI features.

How do I access the AI features?

After updating Windows 11, click the Copilot icon in your taskbar or press Windows key + C to open the AI assistant instantly.

Is my data safe?

Absolutely. Microsoft follows strict privacy standards and your data is processed securely. You control what information Copilot can access.
